Bar Menu

Old Fashioned: A timeless classic, the Old Fashioned is the epitome of a true gentleman's cocktail. Made with a delicate balance of bourbon or rye whiskey, a sugar cube, a few dashes of Angostura bitters, and a twist of citrus rind, this cocktail is served over ice and garnished with a cherry. Its bold yet smooth flavor makes it a beloved choice for whiskey enthusiasts.

Paloma: Refreshing and vibrant, the Paloma is a delightful Mexican cocktail that's perfect for any summer occasion. Crafted with a blend of tequila, freshly squeezed grapefruit juice, lime juice, and a touch of soda, it's often served over ice with a salt-rimmed glass. The Paloma offers a zesty and invigorating taste, with a hint of sweetness that dances on the palate.

Sunset Serenade: Allow your taste buds to embark on a tropical adventure with the Sunset Serenade. A whimsical creation that blends rum, coconut cream, pineapple juice, and a splash of grenadine, this cocktail is shaken to perfection and served over crushed ice. Garnished with a slice of pineapple and a cherry, experience a taste of paradise with every sip.
